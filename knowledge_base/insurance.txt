### Insurance Overview:
Insurance is a financial product that helps protect individuals and organizations from financial loss. It involves paying a premium to an insurer in exchange for protection against certain risks or events, such as accidents, illness, death, or property damage.

### Types of Insurance:
1. **Health Insurance**: Covers medical expenses and health-related costs. It may include doctor visits, hospital stays, surgeries, and preventive care.
2. **Auto Insurance**: Provides financial protection against physical damage or bodily injury resulting from traffic accidents.
3. **Homeowners Insurance**: Protects your home and belongings from damage or theft. It may also cover liability in case someone gets injured on your property.
4. **Life Insurance**: Pays out a lump sum to a designated beneficiary upon the death of the insured person.
5. **Disability Insurance**: Offers income replacement if you become unable to work due to illness or injury.

### Common Insurance Terms:
- **Deductible**: The amount you pay out-of-pocket before your insurance policy kicks in to cover expenses.
- **Claim**: A request made to an insurance company for payment based on a covered event.
- **Exclusion**: Events or situations that are not covered by an insurance policy.
